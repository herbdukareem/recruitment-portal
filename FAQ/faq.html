Your picture needs to meet the following specifications: it should not be more than 200KB in file size, must have exact dimensions of 600x600 pixels, should be taken with a white or off-white background, must be in one of the following formats—JPEG, JPG, or PNG, and it must be in color.

You can use a variety of software to resize your picture, with Microsoft Paint being a typical option as it comes installed on every Windows computer. To resize your photo in Paint, open the photo file, click on Resize in the Ribbon to bring up the Resize and Skew dialog box, uncheck Maintain aspect ratio under the Resize section, select Pixels under the By options, type 600 in both the Horizontal and Vertical boxes, click OK, and finally save the file.

The Biodata section must be filled in first before other sections can be filled. Complete the Biodata section filling in every required filled on the form and then click Save and Continue to advance to the next section.

Any of the modern and up to date browsers (Internet Explorer, Chrome, Firefox) is appropriate for use.

We offer a wide range of career opportunities for both Graduate Trainees and Experienced Professionals. If you are 28 years old or younger and have graduated from a University, Polytechnic, or Monotechnic within the last five years, click on the 'Graduate Trainees' button on the application portal to view the eligibility criteria for entry-level jobs. If you are 37 years old or younger and have at least nine years of work experience, click on the 'Experienced Professionals' button to view the eligibility criteria for experienced-hire positions.

Disciplines are listed based on available vacancies. Only applications in listed disciplines will be considered.

Each applicant is required to submit one job application only. Submission of applications for two or more job positions from a single applicant will attract disqualification of all submitted applications.

Please click on the ‘How to apply’ link at the top right corner of the application portal to view guidelines on how to apply.

Only positions that are currently vacant will be displayed. It is advised that you keep checking the website for future updates.
